This privacy policy describes how JapaneseKanjiDesigns ("we", "our", "us") handles information in connection with our Pinterest application. This application is operated for personal use to manage Pinterest content for JapaneseKanjiDesigns.

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data from users. The only data accessed through the Pinterest API is limited to our own Pinterest business account for the purpose of creating and managing pins.

We do not access or store any data from other Pinterest users.
